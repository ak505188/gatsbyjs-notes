### Beasting Bay: Anchorage
Kill up to 2 encounters using Wind S and Light S:

- Long ear lloris + 2 lloris: Break long ear with Throne Dagger x2 then S stone + Ruinous Kick x2
- Frog II + Fly: Break Frog II with Agnea Dagger x2, Break Fly with Throne Sword x2, then S stone + Fireball x2
- Slug + Fly: Staff x2 Slug, Sword x2 Fly, S stone + Ruinous Kick Fly
- 2 Frog II: Break both then Light S
- Frog II + 2 Frog I: Break Frog II with Dagger x2 then Light S
- Slug + 2 Frog I: Sword x2 or Staff x2 Slug, S stone

1st 3 battles give enough JP, 4th only if 1T, 5&6 need an extra battle.

> If you get a Cait/Puff, kill with Ice M and pick & use Light M on next screen.

### Western Merry Hills Pass
> #### 1 Cait or 2 Puff
Will need to kill 2 Merry Hills battles (preferably day) and up to 2 Sai battles. Might still be short afterwards, depending on what Merry Hills battles you got. Worst battles leave you short about 100. 
> #### 1 Cultured Cait
Take 1 day 1 night battle. Might need Sai battles.
> #### 1 Queen Puff
Post Merry Hills battle, want Osvald to have >= **1522JP** (Assuming T1 kill bonus). If short, go tag Sai early and kill stuff on the way. Then teach Osvald Extra Experience. Will want to take day Merry Hills battles. Will likely need both Sai battes for EXP as well, depends on Merry Hills groups. With day Merry Hills and night Sai should have enough.
> #### Cait + Puff
Can take night Merry Hills, but if worst battle will need 2 Sai battles.
> #### 1 Queen Puff + 1 Puff
Extra Experience Assassins, and possibly 1 Sai battle covers it. Can opt for night Merry Hills, but if worst battle will need 2 Sai battles.
> #### King Puff
Extra Experience, and 1/2 Sai battle should be enough.
>
> Anything not mentioned doesn't need anything extra.
> No Caits/Puffs does BG
